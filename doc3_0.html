Blood Retention Rate

Blood Retention Status

  • As of 00:00 every day
  • Current blood reserves = stock available to medical institutions + stock waiting for testing
  • (Blood waiting for examination: Blood that can be supplied to medical institutions after completion of examination)
  • Proper amount of blood: 5 days or more on average per day

Blood Supply and Demand Crisis Stage

  • Attention (Blue) Signs of lack of blood supply and demand, red blood cell preparations for less than 5 days a sign-monitoring activity
  • Caution (Yellow) partial shortage of blood supply, less than 3 days of red blood cell preparations coordinated operation
  • Boundary (Orange) Lack of blood supply and demand continues, red blood cell preparations for less than 2 days Check the contrast plan
  • Serious (Red) Increased blood supply and demand, less than 1 day of red blood cell preparations Ready to respond immediately
